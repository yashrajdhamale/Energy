Welcome to the Energy Usage Tracker

In this project, we have used the IoT (Internet of Things) technology to meticulously monitor the energy consumption of a infrastructure over the course of a week for multiple devices.

To monitor the energy consumption, we have used the Tuya smart energy monitor socket with the advantage of monitoring the volatge,current and power of certain IT and Non-IT devices.

By using the Tuya API, we have separately collected the data for seven days in seven separate files and displayed them on this webpage. We have utilized this data to generate energy trend charts.

Whether anyone is managing IT loads, non-IT loads, or monitoring daily datasets, our dashboard provides a comprehensive view to empower your energy management decisions.
